Fluxionum

A puzzle game that teaches calculus.

Free and runs in the browser.

Playtesters wanted!

Play the game and then fill out a playtester feedback form. You do not need to be a calculus student to playtest, all feedback is much appreciated.

Learn by playing

Build intuition for calculus with visual and interactive puzzles. Topics covered: linear functions, power rule, sine and cosine, sum, product, and chain rules.

Practice and master the derivative

Practice taking derivatives with unlimited attempts. A building block system simplifies algebra, without making things too easy.

Video solutions when you get stuck

Each puzzles has a video that explains the solution and the math behind it. You can use these videos as a hint if you get stuck on a puzzle or as a summary after you solve it.
